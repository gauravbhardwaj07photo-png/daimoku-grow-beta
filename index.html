The Peepal Tree (Main Practice)

Known as the Bodhi tree under which Shakyamuni attained enlightenment. It represents your main daily practice. Grows from a tiny Seed to a Majestic Tree based on your progress towards your tree target hours.

The Lotus Flower (First Active Target)

Sprouts on the left soil. Symbolizes the principle of "simultaneity of cause and effect" and the ability to bloom pure and beautiful out of muddy waters. Grows, opens, and blooms as you complete Target 1.

The Bamboo Shoot (Second Active Target)

Sprouts on the right soil. Represents resilience, flexibility, and rapid growth—bending gracefully in the wind but never breaking. Grows tall and sprouts sections as you complete Target 2.

Red Apple Fruits (Advanced Milestones)

Grow on the outer branches of the Majestic Tree once you surpass your target hours (1 apple per 3 hours 20 mins). Symbolizes the actual proof, benefits, and fruits of your long-term practice.
